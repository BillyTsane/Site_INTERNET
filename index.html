African Conference of Mathematics and Applications will take place during November 20-23, 2024, in Niamey, Niger. The conference areas of focus will be in mathematics and its applications. The said conference is a high profile conference organized by the commission for research of the African Mathematical Union Commission of Research and Innovation ( African Mathematical Union (africamathunion.org) )in collaboration with the local Mathematics team from Abdou Moumouni University. The conference's goal is to bring together academics from all across Africa and the diaspora to share the most recent findings and developments in pertinent subjects. It also offers the perfect setting for forming fresh scientific partnerships. By bringing together top researchers, international communities, and business leaders to discuss the most recent advancements and innovations in the fields of physics and mathematics, the conference aims to promote high-quality research and foster a culture of genuine international cooperation between mathematicians and research professionals. In more concentrated sessions with cutting-edge presentations and in-person engagement with industry executives and experts, ACOMA will boost the chances for researchers to network with colleagues from around Africa. During the conference, there will be both paper and poster presentations.
